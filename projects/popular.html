20 POPULAR GAMES OF THE DECADE

The world has been swimming in gaming ecstasy for the past decade. The market is full of an array of games in sorts of modes and design, all to indulge gamers in their fantasy worlds. Over the decade we have seen some amazing games. Some of them have been inducted in the gaming hall of fame. Here are some of the decade’s biggest games

  1. The Legend of Zelda

    Genre: Action Adventure

    Rating: E10+

    Game modes: Single-player, Multi-player

    About: The legend of Zelda is a series of fantasy action adventure games published by the gaming giants Nintendo. The story tells of the hero Link on a mission to save the Princess Zelda and the Kingdom of Hyrule from various enemies. Selling over 75 million copies it has proved to be a favorite among gamers.

  2. Assassin’s Creed

    Genre: Action Adventure

    Rating: PG13

    Game modes: Single player, Multi player

    About:The plot unravels about a struggle between Templars who acquire peace with control and the assassins fighting for peace and free will. Desmond Miles the protagonist and his counterparts continue to capture gamers with every new Ubisoft sequel release. The latest addition to the sequel assassin’s creed syndicate received good reviews.

  3. Grand Theft Auto

    Genre: Action Adventure

    Rating: M

    Game Modes: Single player, Multiplayer

    About: The grand theft auto franchise has been one of the most successful games ever made selling over 250 million copies worldwide. The game and it sequels, involves a player taking up the profile of a criminal seeking to climb through the ranks. By completing missions, the player gets rewarded and moves to the next task in the missions for the criminal kingpins. These games are set in different major cities in every sequel

  4. Resident Evil

    Genre: Action Adventure, Survival, Horror

    Rating: M

    Game Modes: Single player, Multiplayer

    About: Resident evil is popular in the survival horror subgenre worldwide. The storyline follows the struggles of a hero, a special law enforcement member, in eliminating zombies and biohazard experiments from ravaging Raccoon city. The player will fight the umbrella corporation and their devious biochemical creations. The various abilities are determined by the officer to choose to play

  5. Silent Hill

    Genre: Survival horror

    Rating: M

    Game Modes: Single player

    About: The game and its sequel introduces us to different protagonists all lured to the forces in the small town of silent hill. The games make the player engage with the Silent Hill cult that is a dangerous demonic powered antagonist. The player gets to engage in solving riddles and killing of antagonists. The game has sold over 3 million copies worldwide.

  6. Alien: isolation

    Genre: Action Adventure, survival Horror, Sci-fi

    Rating: M

    Game Modes: Single Player

    About: published by Sega back in 2014, the game won the PC Gamer “Game of the year award”. The player takes up Amanda Ripley’s profile in exploring a space station, completing missions, while outsmarting the space station’s resident alien monster and other dangerous human occupants. The antagonist monster remains the same in the whole game and cannot be easily killed.

  7. Super smash bros

    Genre: Action, combat, 2D, 3D

    Rating: E10+

    Game Modes: Single player, Multi Player

    About: this game is among Nintendo’s most classic game. The main objective of the player is to knock the opponent off the stage. The player can employ successive blows to reduce the opponent’s life thus winning the game and proceeding to the next level. Enjoy power ups and smash runs to defeat your enemies.

  8. Angry Birds

    Genre: Puzzle, action, General

    Rating: E

    Game Modes: Single Player

    About: Developed by Rovio entertainment, the narrative behind this game is to use the multi-colored birds to aim and knock out the green colored pigs. Using a slingshot, the player launches attacks on the pigs who are usually sheltered by wood, stone, or glass in order to retrieve stolen eggs. The game also offers power-ups and enough levels to keep the player happy.

  9. Flappy Bird

    Genre: Arcade

    Rating: E3+

    Game Modes: Single player

    About: Developed and published by dotGEARS, as a player the goal is to direct the bird called Faby to move through “Mario type Columns”. Faby is not to touch any of the columns otherwise the player losses the game. Medals are awarded at the end of the game, before proceeding to other levels.

  10. Fruit Ninja

    Genre: Arcade

    Rating: E

    Game Modes:Single player, Multiplayer

    About: This simple game developed by Halfbrick, is a favorite from children to adults. The aim of the game is to slice all fruit thrown onto the screen. The more fruit you are able to slice the more points you get. Occasionally bombs might be dropped in, if sliced, the game is over and the player has to restart the level. The game has over 300 million downloads worldwide.

  11. Farmville

    Genre: Simulation, Role playing, social media games

    Rating: E3+

    Game Modes: Single player with multiple player interactions

    About: Produced and developed by Zynga in 2009, the game has been popularized by social media platforms like Facebook. The game employs the everyday life of running a farm. The player is involved in the planting, harvesting of crops, livestock rearing, and generally the upkeep of the farm. It is a very involving and exciting game.

  12. Roller coaster tycoon

    Genre: Construction Management Simulation

    Rating: E

    Game Modes: Single player

    About: the objective of the player in this game is to manage the construction, development and overall maintenance of an amusement park. The player gets to control locations, scenery and the prices which will keep the amusement park running. The game has had a few sequels over the years and the game still manages to entice gamers all over the world.

  13. Jurassic park: Operation Genesis

    Genre: Construction and Management simulation

    Rating: T

    Game Modes: Single Player

    About: the was game developed by Blue Tongue entertainment in 2003. Operating as the first fully running 3D action simulator, the game offers players the chance of creating the most versatile dinosaur themed park. The game also allows for the creation and feeding of the dinosaurs to keep them happy. You can choose to engage in missions or continue in building the theme park.

  14. Temple run

    Genre: platform, Arcade, Endless runner

    Rating: E

    Game Modes: Single player

    About: temple run is a game released in 2013 by its developers and publishers Imangi Studios. To play this game the player has to direct the runner while avoiding different obstacles along the way. The game is haunted by a weird demonic monkey, sharp corners and obstacles that will make you fall down to your death. The more you run, the more coins and power-ups you collect.

  15. Super Mario run

    Genre:Platform, Endless runner

    Rating: E

    Game Modes:Single player, Multiplayer

    About: This game can be described as a side- scrolling game under the endless run genre, developed and published by Nintendo. With three principal modes of play, the game proves entertaining to all ages. The three modes of play included are the Mushroom kingdom, toad rally and the core game where Mario is the main focus.

  16. Final Fantasy

    Genre:Role playing

    Rating: T

    Game Modes:Single player, Multiplayer

    About:Since 1987, the sequels of this games are in demand so much that a new one is published almost every year. The game is set in different settings and may include different characters with each sequel fighting off evil. The game has released more than 14 sequels and we expect more to be published in years to come.

  17. League of Legends

    Genre: Multiple online arena battle

    Rating: T

    Game Modes: Multiplayer

    About: The league of Legends has received massive popularity amassing over 100 million players monthly as of 2016. The game encompasses the player to summon and defeat the opponent. The goal is to acquire and destroy the opponent’s nexus located at the base of his heart. The current running modes of the game include howling abyss, summoner’s rift, and twisted treeline. All with their own specifications.

  18. Pokémon

    Genre: monster collecting, Role playing,

    Rating: E

    Game Modes:Single player, Multiplayer

    About: Another game by Nintendo, Pokémon depending on the generation you are playing, has recently been getting attention especially after the introduction of Pokémon on the go. The aim of the game is to create an avatar that trains, catches, trades and battles other Pokémon characters walking around. It has been downloaded over 500 million times.

  19. War Craft

    Real-time strategy, Fantasy, sci-fi

    Rating: T

    Game Modes:Single player, Multiplayer

    About:Another massive hit from Blizzard entertainment. the game involves players taking roles as humans invading the orcs or inhabitants of the planet Azeroth. The aim of the game is to destroy the enemy camp especially in multiplayer settings. The single player is more about accomplishing missions.

  20. Star craft

    Genre: Real time strategy, sci-fi

    Rating: T

    Game Modes:Single player, Multiplayer

    About: this game is another popular game from Blizzard entertainment. The storyline of the game shows the epic struggle between zerg, " Xel'Naga, Protoss and Terran dominance in the universe. This game is very addictive.
