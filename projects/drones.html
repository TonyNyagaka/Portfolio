The Best Drones the Market has to Offer

There is a lot of information out there about the finest drones available in the market and hence it can be confusing for a drone enthusiast to figure out which drones actually take the prize.

This article breaks down the best choices of drones meant for different purposes while still putting the highest quality, popularity and features into consideration.

The best camera drones

DJI Phantom 4

All pundits agree that this is DJI’s finest innovation in the technology of drones and I would argue that this by far the best camera drone .If you are looking for the best professional videos then this is the drone for you; it offers a 4k video that comes with impeccable 12 Megapixel photos.

While this drone is easy to fly, it also boasts of unmatched power and pace. Some other important features of this drone include: obstacle avoidance; this allows the drone avoid obstruction while it is moving, approximately 24 minutes of flight and it can be controlled using a smart phone.

All this amazing features that make the DJI Phantom stand out among its peers will cost you a good sum; as it stands the drone goes for $ 1,399.

DJI Phantom 3 Professional

The Phantom 3 has to be one of the most popular drones for those who enjoy piloting drones as a hobby.

It can be easily be controlled by pretty much everyone because it has the ‘’Follow me’’ feature that prevents it from straying away and also the ability to land automatically when the flight time is over.

The Phantom 3 Professional also boasts of 4K video quality, ultrasonic sensors, GPS waypoints, point-of-interest, optical-flow, a 20 minute battery life among many other features.

This drone will cost you $1,128 inclusive of VAT online. You can also consider the advanced make at $903 or even lower and maybe the Standard model at $506.

3DR Solo

This drone is perfect when it comes to capturing those extremely difficult but priceless shots any photographer dreams of because it uses the Go Pro Hero 4 in the place of a built-in camera.

It has a lot of similarities with the Phantom 3 only that the user has the ability to upgrade it for better output.

This drone can be acquired for only $ 999 with the option of adding a GoPro Hero and 3DR gimbal for approximately $1900. If this price puts you off, then I recommend the Phantom 3 since it can equally produce high quality videos.

The best racing drones

Some drones have been manufactured with features that are adaptable to the sport of racing which involves a number of competitors. These drones are lighter so that they can move faster and also very hard because of the numerous crashes.

TBS Vendetta

This is by far the best First Person View racing drone in the market. It is packaged ready to fly but you will need to purchase FPV goggles for an immersive experience, a charger, battery and a controller.

The TBS Vendetta just like other racing drones is tiny in size but it can spectacularly shoot to a speed of 70mph or even more while on a track, this can even go up past 100mph when nose diving.

This amazing quadcopter can be purchased at a fair price of approximately $500.

Immersion RC Vortex 250 Pro

The stand-out feature of this ready-to-fly quadcopter is that it has two instead of three or four bladed propellers. The two blades have an advantage of adding efficiency of the drone while three blades provide more power.

The best drone for beginners

When learning how to pilot a drone you will realize that there is a lot of crashing involved. This might result in you spending a lot of money on repairs and maintenance; it is important to know which drone can withstand a lot of crashing with minimum damage while still being cost-effective.

UDI U818A

Beginners will find the UDI U818A easy to operate because it has makes flight control simpler. The four-loop style tough frame of the drone ensures that the rotors are not damaged in the case of a crash.

The drone goes for an affordable price of $47.99 from Amazon and the rotors are also easy to replace in case they are damaged; they cost no more than $3 a set.

The quadcopter also comes with a 640 x 480-pixel camera to record videos and a flight life of 8 minutes.

The best cheap drone

If you can comfortably pilot a drone but you are looking for one that posses most of the features but is still affordable, then you should go for the Blade Nano QX RTF.

Blade Nano QX RTF

This drone can fly faster and is easier to operate compared to other drones that are available in the market for cheaper prices.

The standard Nano QX RTF make sells for no more than $100 while the Nano QX FPV RTF make which contains a FPV headset goes for above $300.

It has a flight life of 8 minutes.

Conclusion

So there you have it, I hope you now have a clear idea of the finest drones the market has to offer and as such it should not be a problem figuring out your preference.
